Our Bills, My Payment.

Often times, living with others can become difficult when it comes to expenses. Our goal is to not only make paying and splitting bills easier, but to help with more on time payment.

Who We Are

We are an all-in-one platform for checking and paying bills, IOUs, and even uploading and splitting grocery receipts.

What We Do

We help with the hassle of tracking down roomies to obtain payments they owe. All it takes is a simple push of a button thanks to our app.

Wait, There's More

MyPayment can be used for weeklong vacations or weekend getaways. Use our app to split costs between friends for your upcoming spring break trip!
