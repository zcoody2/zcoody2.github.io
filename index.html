Rent Splitting Made Simple.

MyPayment

Having trouble splitting rent with your roommates? Well it's now been made easy with the help of our new app, MyPayment!

What do we do?

With the help of MyPayment, roommates are now able to split and pay expenses without the hassle of having to physically look for fellow roomies! All it takes is the simple step of directing bills through the app or even uploading receipts from trips to the grocery store!

MyPayment Vacation Edition

No roommates but need MyPayment for a vacation? No problem! Use MyPayment to split bills between friends for a spring break trip!

MyPayment Channels

Share bills and IOUs. Totally free for web, iPhone, and Android.
